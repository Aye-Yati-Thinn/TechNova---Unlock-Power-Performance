General Warranty Policy

  • Minimum 1-year carry-in warranty.
  • Battery covered for 1 year only.
  • On-site pickup/drop-off available every Wednesday & Saturday via 3PL.
  • Warranty excludes third-party damage and physical wear & tear.

Warranty Exclusions

  • Damage from accident, abuse, misuse, or improper storage.
  • External causes like power surges, environmental factors, or unauthorized repairs.
  • Alterations or modifications by unauthorized parties.
  • Damage from incompatible accessories or software issues.
  • Products not purchased from ABEX or its authorized resellers.
  • Shipping damage not caused by carrier.
  • System corruption, malware, or improper software settings.
